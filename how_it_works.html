Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

What is YOLOv8 and why is it special?

YOLOv8 (You Only Look Once) is a state-of-the-art object detection model that can identify multiple objects in images and video in real-time. It's special because it achieves high accuracy while maintaining incredible speed, making it perfect for real-time applications.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All uploaded images are processed securely and never shared with third parties. Your personal information is encrypted and stored safely in our MongoDB database. You can delete your data at any time.

What image formats are supported?

We support all major image formats including PNG, JPG, JPEG, and WEBP. For webcam streaming, we support real-time video capture from any connected camera.

Can I use this for commercial purposes?

Yes! FireEye AI is perfect for commercial applications including security systems, retail analytics, inventory management, and more. Contact us for enterprise solutions.

How accurate is the detection?

Our YOLOv8 model achieves up to 98% accuracy on standard object detection benchmarks. However, accuracy can vary based on image quality, lighting conditions, and object visibility.
